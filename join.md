## InnoCORE Fellow Recruitment

The **POLAR-AI Research Center** is actively seeking extraordinary postdoctoral researchers to drive the future of extreme environment robotics. We aim to recruit around 25 researchers annually, with a special focus on acquiring more than 9 global postdocs per year.

### Qualifications and Fields
We are looking for experts in:
*   **G1 (Mobility):** RL walking, Robot Manipulation, Autonomous planning, AI Perception, Mechanical Design.
*   **G2 (Sensors):** Physical AI and PHM, IR Sensors, Deep Learning for Anomaly Detection, Nano-tactile sensors.
*   **G3 (Energy):** Battery Thermal Management, Fluid/Thermal Dynamics (CFD), Energy autonomous systems.
*   **G4 (Integration):** Ad-hoc networking, Systems Engineering, ROS2 Architecture, Remote Control.

### Benefits and Welfare
*   **Competitive Salary:** Base salary of ~90 Million KRW/year + additional performance-based incentives.
*   **Dedicated Support:** Dedicated administrative manager for visa, housing, and general settlement to ensure you can focus exclusively on research.
*   **Top-Tier Infrastructure:** Access to GIST's HPC-AI infrastructure and our extensive 10B KRW suite of equipment.

### Career Growth Roadmap
*   Opportunities to transition to **Research Assistant Professor** or tenure-track positions.
*   Technology transfer and startup spin-off tracks.
*   **Multi-Mentoring System:** Cross-mentoring from faculty across various institutions, globally renowned scholars, and industry R&D executives.

### How to Apply
Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. Please send your CV, a brief cover letter, and a list of your top 3 publications to our administrative office.
