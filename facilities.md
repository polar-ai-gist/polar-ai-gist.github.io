Our center utilizes world-class testing and validation infrastructure (approximately 85 major devices worth ~10 Billion KRW) distributed across multiple core facilities.

## Demonstration Infrastructure

1.  **GIST In-house Environmental Testing Lab**
    *   **Capabilities:** Environmental chamber capable of reaching -40°C.
    *   **Features:** Ice surface simulation panels and network emulators.
2.  **KOPRI Ultra-low Temperature Room (-80°C)**
    *   Used for extreme cold-shock validation for robotic joints and sensor viability testing.
3.  **KRISO Ice Model Basin (32m x 32m)**
    *   Provides vast, controlled ice environments to test gait stability and multi-robot navigation at scale.
4.  **Daegwallyeong Outdoor Demonstration Site**
    *   The ultimate proving ground for 24-48 hour full-system continuous operation in harsh winter conditions.

## Major Equipment

*   **Computing:** High-performance GPU servers and HPC clusters for Physics-informed AI and SLAM.
*   **Robotics:** Multiple humanoid robots, wearable robots, 6-axis collaborative manipulators, and autonomous drones.
*   **Sensors & Optics:** Spectrometers, high-resolution mid-IR cameras, and extensive motion capture systems.
